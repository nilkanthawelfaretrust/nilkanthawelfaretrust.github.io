Nilkantha Welfare Trust is formed by a group of individuals of compassionate minds. They are true to their obligation to give back to society its due share by lending hands to the poor especially, the deprived. An organization is driven by a single goal which is to do our part in making the world a better place for all. Our decision-making process is informed by comprehensive empirical studies and high-quality data evaluation. Situated in front of the statue of Devon Ke Dev...Mahadev and Lord Krishna . Devotees chant God’s names in the form of the maha-mantra, or the great prayer for deliverance: Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare/Hare Rama Hare Rama, Rama Rama, Hare Hare, and OM NAMAH SHIVAAY. This teaches that the ultimate goal for all living beings is to reawaken their love for God, or Lord Krishna. This is the government trust that works on the development of the poor and needy people of our society. Mainly concentrating on the development of their life and to fulfill their basic needs by promoting social awareness campaign to help the poor sections of society. This includes free food distribution to all visitors, free medical checkups, purified water, and also residential facilities at minimum rates.
Our Mission is to extend a helping hand to the poor and the needy, the deprived and the destitute ones.
To dedicate this charitable trust to the temple of MAHADEV and RADAHA-KRISHNA.
To bring the members closer together for the purpose of teaching a simpler and more natural way of life.
Free medical check-ups, providing purified water for all the visitors and thus making it safe in all emergencies.
Unlimited food for all the devotees,Social awareness camps to promote help to the poorer section of society.
To not tolerate any discrimination against any caste, religion or creed.
All humans will be treated equally.
Residential facilities for the needy people.
SERVICE OF GOD IN MAN.
To bring about Sustainable Development by providing the basic necessities of life. To provide free food and water so that no stomach go hungry. To promote relief camp and free medical check ups and residential areas for the needy people.
All we want to dedicate our company in – "Jiva seba" in the sense of "Shiva Seba" for the upbringing of the needy people so that they live a better life
